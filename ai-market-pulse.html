Project Description

AI Market Pulse Dashboard is a modular, AI powered market analysis platform designed for professional users. It provides automated data pipelines, interactive visualizations, and AI generated insights for financial markets. Its architecture is fully extensible, allowing adaptation to other domains such as digital marketing, IoT, or social media analytics. The platform demonstrates enterprise grade automation, real time intelligence, and seamless integration of multiple forecasting and AI models.
